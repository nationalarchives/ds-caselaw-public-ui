Thank you for submitting your licence application.

Here’s what happens next:

  • We will send you a copy of your application to your main contact’s email address shortly.
  • Your confirmation email will also contain your application number, please use this if you need to contact us about your application.
  • The licensing team will review your application and if they need any further information, we will contact you by email.
  • The Discovery board is a cross-departmental board at The National Archives that grants licences. The board meets every month so it may be a few weeks until you get a decision.
  • We will email you with the board’s decision as soon as it has been made.
  • In the future we may contact you to invite you to take part in research. All participation is voluntary and will not impact your application or licence terms in any way.

If you have any questions, you can email the licensing team at: caselawlicence@nationalarchives.gov.uk.
