About Find Case Law

What Find Case Law does

Find Case Law is a service that provides public access to court judgments and tribunal decisions made in England and Wales from 2001 onwards.

It publishes selected judgments and decisions on this website, in a range of formats.

It is provided by The National Archives, working in partnership with the Ministry of Justice, His Majesty's Courts and Tribunals Service, and the Judicial Office.

Please note that:

  • Find Case Law is currently in beta, meaning it is being actively developed and may change in order to test and introduce new features
  • Find Case Law is not a complete record of judgments and decisions (see our Publishing policy)
  • Find Case Law does not provide legal advice, interpretation or support. Sources of these include:
    • Citizens Advice
    • The Law Centres Network
    • Support Through Court
    • Advocate
